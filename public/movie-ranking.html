The Best Movies According to Matthew

My top 3 movies of all-time

  1. Star Wars

    Star Wars is an epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas.
    The original film, released in 1977, became a worldwide pop-culture phenomenon.

  2. Inception

    Inception is a 2010 science fiction action film written and directed by Christopher Nolan.
    It stars Leonardo DiCaprio as a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious.

  3. Harry Potter

    Harry Potter is a series of fantasy novels written by British author J. K. Rowling.
    The series chronicles the adventures of a young wizard, Harry Potter, and his friends Hermione Granger and Ron Weasley, all of whom are students at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
